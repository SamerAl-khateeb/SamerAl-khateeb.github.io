Projects

  • Visualizing Powerful Actors of Deviant Cyber Flash Mobs (DCFMs) of ISIL on Twitter [For Information Visualization Class]

    I created a visualization that answer four basic questions: (1) Who are the powerful nodes/actors? (2) Where are the powerful nodes/actors? (3) What is the growth of the network over time? (4) What is the growth of the network, in a location, over time? I used two tools to demonstrate that (1) Tableau Public, (2) Google Fusion Tables

  • Topic Modeling For Associated Press Articles Using Latent Dirichlet Allocation [For Information Science Theories Class]

    I did topic modeling for the Associated Press Articles using the LDA Download The Project Report, Download The Project Presentation.

  • UAMS Family Map Inventories Survey System [For Software Engineer Class]

    I worked with a team of four students on this project. Our goal was to create a simple basis for a survey system the UAMS Family Map Inventories would later adapt and use to replace their current paper filing system. Our project was accepted, and is currently being adapted for their systems.

  • A Comparative Analysis of Digital Filtering for Seismic Signals from Several Earthquakes in Turkey [Undergraduate Capstone Project]

    I worked with Dr. Coskun Bayrak who is a professor at the Department of Computer Science at UALR. My goal was to compare the performance of three kinds of digital filters using earthquakes data collected from three cities at Turkey. The result of the project is going to be published in the near future. Download The Project Presentation
